About AI Tools Galaxy

AI Tools Galaxy is a simple website designed to help students find useful AI tools faster.

Instead of overwhelming users with huge lists, this site focuses on practical tools that help with homework, essays, studying, note-taking, and presentations.

Who this site is for

This site is mainly built for students and beginners who want to study smarter, write better, and save time using AI tools.

Our goal

The goal is to make AI tools easy to understand, easy to find, and actually useful in real school situations.
