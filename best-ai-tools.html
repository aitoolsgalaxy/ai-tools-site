FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about AI tools.

What are the best AI tools for beginners?

ChatGPT, Grammarly, Canva, and Perplexity are some of the easiest AI tools to start with because they are simple to use and useful right away.

Which AI tools are best for writing?

ChatGPT and Grammarly are strong choices for writing drafts, rewriting text, improving clarity, editing grammar, and making text more polished.

Which AI tools are best for students?

Students often benefit most from ChatGPT, Canva, Perplexity, and Grammarly because these tools support writing, studying, research, and presentations.

Do all of these tools cost money?

Not always. Many tools offer free plans, limited free access, or trial options that are enough for beginners or lighter use.

What is the best AI tool overall?

For most users, ChatGPT is one of the best all-around AI tools because it can help with writing, explanations, brainstorming, and productivity in many different situations.

How should I pick between multiple AI tools?

Choose based on your main task. If you need writing help, start with ChatGPT or Grammarly. If you need design, use Canva. If you need research, start with Perplexity.
