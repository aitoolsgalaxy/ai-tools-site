How to choose a free AI tool

The best free AI tool is usually the one that solves one clear problem without adding friction to your workflow.

1

Pick one task first

Start with a clear goal like writing, grammar correction, research, slide creation, or organizing notes.

2

Test one tool properly

Use the tool on a real task instead of just trying random prompts. Compare the output to your normal workflow.

3

Keep what saves time

If the tool clearly saves effort and produces useful results, keep it. If not, move on and test another free option.
